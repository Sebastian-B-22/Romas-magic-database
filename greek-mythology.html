ğŸ“– Quick Reference Cards

The Big Three

Zeus (Sky), Poseidon (Sea), Hades (Underworld) - The three most powerful gods who divided the world after defeating the Titans.

The 12 Olympians

Zeus, Hera, Poseidon, Demeter, Athena, Apollo, Artemis, Ares, Aphrodite, Hephaestus, Hermes, and either Hestia or Dionysus (depends on the source!).

The Titans

The older generation of gods who ruled before the Olympians. Led by Kronos. Defeated in the Titanomachy and imprisoned in Tartarus.

Mount Olympus

The home of the gods! The highest mountain in Greece. The gods live in palaces at the summit, above the clouds.

Tartarus

The deepest pit of the Underworld. A prison for the worst villains and the defeated Titans. So deep it would take 9 days to fall to the bottom!

The Fates

Three sisters who control destiny: Clotho (spins the thread of life), Lachesis (measures it), Atropos (cuts it). Even gods can't change fate!
